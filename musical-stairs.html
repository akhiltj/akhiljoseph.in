Musical Stairs Illuminate Kochi Metro Stations with Interactive Melodies

Step into Harmony Kerala's First Musical Stairs

Step into a world where every footfall creates a melody and lights up your path. Introducing Kochi Metro's first musical stairs, a pioneering project in Kerala that blends technology with art. Each step on these innovative stairs triggers through-beam photo sensors, activating a strip of light and playing a musical note. This interactive installation not only adds a playful, engaging element to the daily commute but also promotes physical activity by encouraging the use of stairs.

Imagine the joy of hearing a delightful tune and seeing a bright light with every step you take. The musical stairs in Kochi Metro are designed to transform an everyday activity into a magical experience. Whether you're a daily commuter or a visitor, these stairs invite you to create your own symphony and add a spark of joy to your journey.

This project is more than just an artistic installation; it’s a step towards integrating fun and fitness into urban mobility. By encouraging people to choose the stairs over escalators and elevators, it fosters a healthier lifestyle while also making the metro station a more vibrant and engaging space. Come, light up your journey and create a symphony with every step you take on Kerala’s first musical stairs.

  • Client : Kochi Metro
